Onboarding overview

Onboarding enables a new customer to use to scan credentials. Once a new customer signs an agreement with to use , that customer can register organizations under their account. Organizations will manage the credentials needed to enable the Verify mobile apps to scan holder credentials.

Key onboarding concepts

  • The customer manages organizations.

  • Customers use organizations to manage credentials used by verifiers.

  • To enable the Verify mobile app to scan holder credentials, verifiers must use a credential issued by an organization.

Future releases

May 2021 note: In future releases, the system will capture metering information, aggregated information about scan counts. Organizations will use these metrics in monitoring. will use the metrics to tie into a billing system. Using this, can charge customers based on usage.
