Completing standard administration

Before proceeding with verifier preparation, work with your business's team to make sure that you're ready. Your team uses the administration console to complete most administrative tasks.

Deployment, setup

After adds your business as a customer, your team completes standard administration and setup. As appropriate to your organization, your Customer Administrators set up organizations and Organization Administrators. In turn, Organization Administrators add verifier credentials, which verifiers use for authentication.

Verifier roles and credentials

At your business, Organization Administrators complete tasks as needed for verifier readiness:

  • Add verifier credentials for the appropriate staff.

  • Distribute verifier credentials to staff on schedule, using the appropriate distribution, for example, either sent by email or printed and posted onsite.
