Deleting a customer

Deleting removes a customer from the solution. This procedure:

  • Inactivates Customer Administrators, and deletes associated users
  • Inactivates the customer's organizations, and deletes associated users
  • Inactivates the customer's verifier credentials

To delete a customer:

  1. Log in to the administrative console.
  2. On the home page, click the Manage customers tile.
  3. On the Customers page, in the row for the customer that you want to delete, under Actions, click the dots icon.
  4. In the Delete Customer pop-up window, click Delete to confirm.
