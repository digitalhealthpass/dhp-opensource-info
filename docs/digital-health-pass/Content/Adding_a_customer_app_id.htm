Adding a customer in App ID

The first step in onboarding a new customer is registration in IBM® Cloud App ID. You'll first gather required customer information. Then, enter this information in App ID. App ID registers authentication and user-specific data for mobile and web apps. After completing this procedure, you'll finish adding the customer using administration.

To add a customer in App ID:

  1. Collect the information listed in the table.
  2. In App ID, enter the information in the required fields.
  3. Continue customer setup in Adding a customer in administration.

For information on roles in the following table, see Roles and responsibilities.
