About customer profiles

With Release 2 of administration, established customer profiles in the product. These profiles include customer-specific information and configurations, for example, verifier configurations.

Current deployment, Q2 2022

In Release 2, customer profiles are accessed only by internal staff. customers cannot view or access their profiles.

Future

In a future release, plans on enhancing customer profiles for customer access. If you have questions about product features and plans, contact the team.
