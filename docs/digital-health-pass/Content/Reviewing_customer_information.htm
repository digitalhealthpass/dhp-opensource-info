Reviewing customer information

Review customer information, for example, to understand the organization setup or to check the assignment of administrative roles.

To review customer information:

  1. Log in to the administrative console.
  2. On the home page, click the Manage customers tile.
  3. On the Customers page, click the customer that you want.
  4. As needed, review information and drill down for the detail that you want. For example, on the Organizations page, click the organization that you want.
  5. As needed, drill down for information on verifier credentials.
