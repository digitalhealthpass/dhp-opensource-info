Process overview

Here's a summary of customer steps in onboarding:

  1. The Customer Administrator receives a "welcome" email from with information for accessing administration.
  2. The Customer Administrator uses the administrative console to set up organizations as needed, to add and manage verifiers.
  3. The Customer Administrator can manage organizations (initially, only the default organization), create new organizations, and manage verifier credentials for each organization.
  4. Verifiers are authenticated per verification session, then use the Verify mobile app to scan holder health passes or cards, and determine their validity.
