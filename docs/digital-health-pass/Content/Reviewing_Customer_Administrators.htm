Reviewing Customer Administrators

Review administrators set up for a selected customer.

To review Customer Administrators:

  1. Log in to the administrative console.
  2. On the home page, click the Manage customers tile.
  3. On the Customers page, in the row for the customer that you want, under Actions, click the person icon.
  4. On the Administrators page, review information for the Customer Administrators.
  5. In the menu that appears, click Administrators....
