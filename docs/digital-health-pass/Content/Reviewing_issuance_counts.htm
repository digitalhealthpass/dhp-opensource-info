Reviewing issuance counts

Review credential issuance counts for a specific time period. By issuer, you can check totals for:

  • Customer (client ID) for which the issuer issues credentials

  • Issued credential type, for example, vaccination or PCR test

  • Credential specification, for example, , Good Health Pass, or Digital COVID Certificate (European Union)

Optionally, you can download this information in spreadsheet (.csv) format.

To review issuance counts:

  1. Log in to the administrative console.
  2. On the home page, click [FORTHCOMING].
  3. To download the information, [FORTHCOMING].
