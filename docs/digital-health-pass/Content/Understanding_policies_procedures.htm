Understanding your organization's policies and procedures

With the solution, verifying organizations — for example, airlines, worksites and entertainment venues — have flexibility to verify credentials based on location-specific criteria. Make sure that you're completing verification appropriately.

Examples

  • In addition to requiring a valid health pass when scanning QR codes, your procedures might require also checking picture ID for added verification.

  • If you scan a health pass that is invalid or expired, your organization might provide specific guidance. For instance, you might be required to share with the pass holder the reason that the pass is invalid, as it appears in the Wallet.

If you're an onsite administrator

If you administer use of at your business, plan your policies and procedures well in advance. Provide adequate support and training, so verifiers know what steps to take if something is wrong with a pass or card.
