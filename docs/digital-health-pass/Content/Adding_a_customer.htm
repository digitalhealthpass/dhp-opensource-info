Adding a customer in administration

After you complete initial setup steps for a new customer using App ID, you'll continue setup using the administrative console.

To add a customer in administration:

  1. Log in to the administrative console.
  2. On the home page, click the Manage customers tile.
  3. On the Customers page, click Customer +.
  4. On the Create Customer page, enter:
    • Customer information: The customer's billing ID and name
    • Customer administrator: The first, last names and a valid email address
  1. Click Add Customer.
