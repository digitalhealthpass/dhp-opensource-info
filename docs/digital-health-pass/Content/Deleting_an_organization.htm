Deleting an organization

Deleting removes an organization from the solution. This procedure:

  • Inactivates the organization, along with associated Organization Administrators
  • Deletes associated users
  • Inactivates the organization's verifier credentials

To delete an organization:

  1. Log in to the administrative console.
  2. On the home page, click the Manage customers tile.
  3. On the Organization page, in the row for the organization that you want to delete, under Actions, click the trash icon.
  4. In the menu that appears, click Delete.
  5. In the Delete Organization pop-up window, click Delete to confirm.
